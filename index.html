Seasons

Autumn

It represents some highs in the midst of the falls, where hope appears vaguely, where feeling is undefined, where everything is colorless, without definition. This is the time to acquire knowledge.

Winter

It represents the great drops of bitcoin, or the great achievements of profit, only those who lived this season know how dark it is, but they also know that it is in it where we find the greatest opportunities to increase our amount of bitcoin.

Spring

Is the most awaited season for those who believe in bitcoin, it is where feelings are the most beautiful, hope is in all hearts, it is the season where we reap the fruits planted.

Summer

It represents the beginning of the discharge, the recovery of faith, where we begin to see the recovery of colors and feelings begin to boil again.
